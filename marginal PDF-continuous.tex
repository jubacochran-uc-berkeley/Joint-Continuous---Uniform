Q 1.3 Marginal PDF

Compute the marginal PDF of Y. 


\begin{proof}

Theorem 1.3.13 For Jointly continuous random variables X and Y with joint PDF f, the marginal PD of Y is:

\[
f_Y(y) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x, y)\, dx \quad \forall y \in \mathbb{R}
\]

\end{proof}
